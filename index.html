A STORY

Bobby Moynihan, had the idea in college where he would go to parties, and they wouldn’t be any bowls.Given a bag of chips, there was only one logical thing he could say, “But why aren’t they in a bowl?” (Even if there were bowls they’d just be regular bowls from the store, not from the internet.) That’s when he got the idea for the critically acclaimed leading bowl company Oyster and Toot©

Always striving for convenience, Oyster and Toot© sends the bowl directly to YOU.

Founder Bobby Moynihan believes that human beings have a wonderful tradition of food and feeding each other,and that’s what bowls are all about.
